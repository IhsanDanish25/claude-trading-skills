# Trading Strategy

## Core Philosophy
Munger principles + Minervini VCP + AI confirmation.
Only buy what makes sense fundamentally AND technically.

## Entry Signals (ALL must be true)
- FTD Score ≥ 80 (confirmed uptrend)
- VCP state: Pre-breakout OR Breakout
- Opus 4.7 confidence ≥ 7/10
- No earnings within 5 days

## Position Sizing
- Risk per trade: 1% of portfolio
- Stop: 7% below entry (hard, placed immediately)
- Trailing stop: 10% for winners
- Max position: 25% of portfolio
- Max simultaneous positions: 2

## Exit Rules
- Stop hit → exit immediately
- 20-25% gain → start trailing 10%
- Fundamental thesis broken → exit regardless of price

## Research Model
- Routine analysis: Claude Opus 4.7
- Deep complex research: Claude Opus 4.8
- Insider tracking: Congress trades + Buffett holdings
